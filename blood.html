Why It Matters

Your gift is irreplaceable

Blood and organ donation are among the most profound acts of human generosity. Every day, people need blood during accidents, surgeries, and treatments. Since blood cannot be synthesised, hospitals depend entirely on voluntary donors.


For patients in organ failure, a donated kidney, heart, liver, or cornea can mean a second chance at life. By donating, you become part of a chain of compassion that touches countless families.

Before You Donate

Are You Eligible?

Basic health criteria ensure safety for both donor and recipient. Contact your nearest blood bank to confirm your eligibility.

🗓 Age above 18 years
⚖️ Weight above 50 kg
🦠 No infectious diseases
💪 Medically fit & healthy
💉 Haemoglobin ≥ 12.5 g/dL
🕐 3 months since last donation
